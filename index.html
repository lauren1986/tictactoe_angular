Tic Tac Toe

A Motivational Game

Instructions

Need a little pick-me-up? Take your mind off your own bullshit by engaging in a game of tic-tac-toe. Why you ask?

  1. It's distracting. Come on, admit it, you need to get outside of your own head right now.
  2. The layout, typography and colors of this game are incredibly calming. You can't deny it; plus it's 53% backed by science.
  3. If you are smart enough to beat your opponent (aka, focus on something else besides your own problems for two seconds), you'll get rewarded with a priceless piece of wisdom that will solve all your woes. True story.

So what are you waiting for? Pick a partner and get playing!
